# Client Onboarding Dossier & Boundary Matrix
*Chiti Technologies — Official Agency Documentation*

## Welcome to Chiti Technologies
You have partnered with a premium design and engineering studio. We specialize in producing immersive, scale-ready digital products utilizing our proprietary V3 Ecosystem architecture. This protocol outlines our engagement structure to ensure a flawless execution of your project roadmap.

---

## 1. The Strategy & Systems Agreement
**Crucial:** Chiti Technologies does not "code from scratch" in isolation. We deploy our enterprise-grade **Unified Design System (UDS)** as the foundation for your product. 
- *Why?* This ensures your product ships to market 40% faster, contains built-in Enterprise accessibility, and guarantees top-tier physics/choreography. 
- Your brand guidelines (Logos, Core Colors, Typography) will be piped directly into our UDS tokens to create a bespoke theme specific to your business without reinventing the wheel.

---

## 2. Required Client Assets (Due Week 1)
To initialize the pipeline, please provide access to the following via our secure portal:
1. **Brand Guidelines:** Master vector logos (.SVG or .AI files only).
2. **Typography Files:** If utilizing custom commercial fonts, provide the `.WOFF2` web font files and licensing.
3. **Domain & DNS Access:** For staging environments and deployment pipelines.
4. **Copywriting / Narrative Documents:** The core message you wish to convey. (If utilizing our UX Writing services, provide a simple bulleted value-proposition list).

---

## 3. The Execution Timeline
We operate in intensive, structured sprints:
- **Phase 1 (Discovery & Token Mapping):** We map your brand identity to our Design System variables and establish the database schemas.
- **Phase 2 (Architectural Wireframing):** Low-fidelity logic maps prioritizing user flows and system state logic. (No aesthetic design applied yet).
- **Phase 3 (Cinematic Injection):** High-fidelity component application. We introduce glassmorphism, Aurora palettes, and physics-driven micro-interactions.
- **Phase 4 (Handoff & UAT):** User Acceptance Testing, QA for i18n scaling, and final deployment.

---

## 4. Communication & Governance
We respect deep work. We do not engage in daily unstructured "syncs."
- **Status Updates:** Delivered asynchronously every Friday via a recorded video walk-through (Loom).
- **Approvals:** Conducted tightly through specified staging links. Once a sprint scope is signed off, any structural changes requested move to a "V2 Backlog" to prevent scope creep and protect the project's launch deadline. This guarantees your product ships on time, every time.
